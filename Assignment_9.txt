2.What are semantic tags? Give a couple of examples.
In HTML semantic tag are tags that describe the meaning or purpose of the content they contain.
semantic tags are used to add meaning to the content of a web page,making it easier for search Engines and other systems to understand the content of a webpage.
ex:<header></header>,<nav></nav>,<footer></footer>,<asid></asid>,<article></article>,
<details></details>.
